Fast Castle Build Order

Esencial

La guía definitiva del Fast Castle que me llevó de 900 a 1200 ELO. Step by step, timing perfecto.

  • 🎯 Timing objetivo: Castle Age @ 16:30
  • 👥 Population: 27+2 build order
  • 🏗️ Buildings: 2 TC + eco perfecto
  • ⚔️ Follow-up: Knights o Unique Units

    • 🔥 Build Order Exacto:

    1. Dark Age: 6 sheep → loom → 4 more on sheep
    2. 11 pop: Build house, 4 to wood
    3. 16 pop: 2 to berries
    4. 17 pop: Boar with scout
    5. 18-19 pop: 2 more to berries (total 4)
    6. 20 pop: Second boar
    7. 21-22 pop: 2 to wood (total 6)
    8. Feudal Age: Click up @ 22 pop
    9. While aging: Build barracks, market
    10. Feudal: 2 gold miners → click Castle
    11. Castle Age: TC + production buildings
